Отправка запроса на доступ

Дата последнего обновления: 2018-05-30
On My access you can file a request for access to different sections/menus in your account and monitor the process of getting the access.
Completing access request form:

а) Fill in the form:

— Find the form using the sections menu, select the legal entity from the list.


— Fill in the form with your details (the form may be partly filled in automatically):

  • “Legal entity” field - select the legal entity from the list;
  • “Company of FESCO Group” field - select one of the companies of FESCO Group;
  • “Name of the signatory” field - enter the signatory’s full name ;
  • “Acting upon” field - indicate the grounds;
  • “Name of the party authorized by the Client to work with documents” – enter the full name of the authorized party;
  • “E-mail address of the party authorized by the Client to work with documents” – enter an e-mail address of the authorized party;
  • “Mobile phone of the party authorized by the Client to work with documents” – enter a mobile phone of the authorized party

— Tick the box “I agree to the processing of my personal data”


— Press “generate and download”;
— Download an Additional Agreement after completing the form;
— Review and sign the Additional Agreement.

б) Uploading supporting documents for getting an access to the electronic archive:

After generating the Additional agreement, you need to provide all the required documents.

— Go to “My rights” on request page of the legal entity:

  • You can get to the section by typing the name of the legal entity in the sections’ menu;
  • Or you will be directed there automatically after sending the documents.


— Find your request for registration, press “upload”:


— You also can download the additional agreement again or cancel the request::

— To upload files press “select files”:

  • You can partly upload the files and upload the rest later, the files will be saved;
  • You also can delete and download files again by pressing the relevant links.


— Press “Submit the documents”.


! If you want to cancel the process of submitting the documents, go to the page of the legal entity in “My rights” section and press “cancel submitting the documents”. * Please note that this option is available for a short period of time.


Updating the request status

— After submitting package of the documents, the request will be processed and after this you will see “request accepted” status;
— Then the request will be sent to an operator, and the status will indicate “the request is being processed”:


— Then the operator handles your request and decides on granting an access:

  • You will get a notification with the information on the operator’s decision on your e-mail when the status changes;
  • You can see the notifications on the upper toolbar as well.
